Who Are You Talking To?

Every device on a network talks to the outside world. But are those conversations safe? OSINT — Open Source Intelligence — means using publicly available information to investigate a threat. No hacking required.

🌍
Real World
Threat intelligence analysts at companies like CrowdStrike and Mandiant do OSINT daily — tracking attacker IPs, domain names, and infrastructure before an attack lands.

Your Tools Today
